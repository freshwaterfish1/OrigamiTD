Oculus Tower Defense Game

Defend your castle from the onslaught of origami creatures in this tower defense game for the Oculus Rift. Using only one button and the rift, build powerful towers to stop the creatures advance. Use physics to your advantage - force the baddies to maze though your towers. Or even seal off paths altogether with unique towers!

Oculus Rift

This game is especially designed for the Oculus rift DK2. Your attention is a recourse, not being able to see the entire battlefield at once means you must constantly be reacting to the current threat and planning for future events.

Unreal Engine 4

With the brand new Unreal Engine 4 under the hood, we can deliver beautiful hand folded visuals for you to enjoy in an immersive virtual reality environment. Unreal also delivers a fully physics based tower defense game, where you must be careful to avoid obstacles between your towers and the monsters trying to get through.
